Wings of fire

Dr.Apj.Abdul kalam

🔥 “Wings of Fire” is an inspiring autobiography of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, former President of India and one of the country’s greatest scientists. The book narrates his journey from a humble childhood in Rameswaram to becoming a leading aerospace scientist and the ‘Missile Man of India’.‘Wings of Fire’ continues to ignite the minds of readers, especially the youth, encouraging them to dream big and achieve their goals with dedication. 🔥
